Methodology
2019

←

→


Methodology, the first book recently published by Martinez Gallery Books, covers the large spectrum of the practice of graffiti through the works of 28 artists from Europe. Beyond disciplines, inhabiting a space between writing, drawing, urban intervention, cartoons, performance and the visual arts, the book shows us how to be a urban writer, while experiencing, exploring and playing.
