GRAFFITI NYC
2006

←

→


Graffiti NYC takes the reader on an "All-City" tour of New York, where graffiti art was born in the early 1970s. Works from the past five years by more than 100 graffiti writers capture the creative excitement of this ephemeral art as it appears in the vast "gallery" of NYC's streets, walls, rooftops, tunnels, trucks, and transit system.
